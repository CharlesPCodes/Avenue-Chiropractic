About Avenue Chiropractic


Dr Muldoon has been a practicing chiropractor since 1992. She is dedicated to restoring the patients’ activities of daily living so they can enjoy recreational activities, can perform work duties, and share in family life, free of pain. This gratifying interaction reconfirms her love for her profession daily.

Dr. Anne Marie Muldoon moved to Northeast Philadelphia at the age of eight years old, and has remained active in the community. She is a member of the Mayfair Business Association. She graduated from Resurrection of Our Lord Catholic Elementary School and Saint Hubert’s Catholic High School for Girls. She remained local throughout her college years, working multiple jobs and attending The Pennsylvania College of Straight Chiropractic, graduating Cum Laude. She continues to foster her education in the field through seminars, primarily focused on myofascial release and restoration of tissue breakdown.
