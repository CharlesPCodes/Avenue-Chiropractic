Welcome to Avenue Chiropractic.
Conveniently located Northeast Philadelphia, we have been providing quality chiropractic care since 1993.

Here at Avenue Chiropractic, we are dedicated to maintaining and restoring our patients’ over all well being through holistic treatments and therapies. As a renowned and reputable local business, we have established working relationships with many local primary care physicians throughout the Philadelphia area. When a patient enters our office they are greeted by our accommodating and energetic staff, whose goal is to provide a warm and friendly atmosphere during treatment. Our treatment plans are individualized to target the patient’s specific pain, weaknesses, and to restore integrity of the musculoskeletal system through chiropractic manipulation and adjunctive therapies. Our overall goal is to restore our patients’ health so they can optimize and enjoy their lives.

Some notable features of our practice include:

  • Affordable massage therapy on sight, including Swedish, deep tissue, and relaxation massage
  • Most insurances accepted
  • Conveniently located near major access roads and SEPTA bus routes
